# 2. Systems of Equations

**Find the values of $x$ and $y$ that satisfy:**
1. $2x + 3y = 12$
2. $x - y = 1$

---

### **Solution: Substitution Method**

**Step 1: Isolate $x$ in Equation 2:**
$$x - y = 1$$

$$x = y + 1$$

> **Note:** We isolate $x$ here because its coefficient is 1, making the substitution cleaner.

---

**Step 2: Substitute into Equation 1:**
$$2x + 3y = 12$$

$$2(y + 1) + 3y = 12$$

---

**Step 3: Solve for $y:$**
$$2y + 2 + 3y = 12$$

$$5y + 2 = 12$$

$$5y = 10$$

**$$y = 2$$**

---

**Step 4: Solve for $x:$**
$$x = y + 1$$

$$x = 2 + 1$$

**$$x = 3$$**

> **Note:** Always plug the solved variable into the simplest available equation to find the second variable.

---

**Step 5: Proving:**
* **Equation 1 Check:**
  $2(3) + 3(2) = 12$
  $6 + 6 = 12$ ✅

* **Equation 2 Check:**
  $3 - 2 = 1$ ✅

---




* **Graphical Intersection:** The solution **(3, 2)** is the precise coordinate where these two lines cross on a Cartesian plane.
[Graphical representation](https://www.geogebra.org/calculator/xzqytcva)



### **Alternative Method: Elimination Method**

**Step 1: Align and prepare the equations**
To eliminate the $y$ variable, we need its coefficients to be additive inverses (e.g., $+3y$ and $-3y$). We keep Equation 1 as is, and multiply the entire Equation 2 by $3$.



* **Original Equations:**
  1) $2x + 3y = 12$
  2) $x - y = 1$

* **Equations for Elimination:**
  1) $2x + 3y = 12$
  2) $3x - 3y = 3$

---


**Step 2: Add the equations**
By adding these two equations, the $+3y$ and $-3y$ cancel each other out:

$$(2x + 3y) + (3x - 3y) = 12 + 3$$

$$5x = 15$$

---


**Step 3: Solve for $x$**
$$x = \frac{15}{5}$$

**$$x = 3$$**

---


**Step 4: Substitute $x$ back to find $y$**
Plug $x = 3$ into Equation 2:
$$3 - y = 1$$
$$-y = 1 - 3$$
$$-y = -2$$

**$$y = 2$$**

---

**Step 5: Proving**
* **Equation 1 Check:**
  $2(3) + 3(2) = 12$
  $6 + 6 = 12$ ✅

* **Equation 2 Check:**
  $3 - 2 = 1$ ✅

---

# 8. EM Spectrum

### Problem Statement

List the following types of electromagnetic radiation in order of **increasing wavelength**: 
Infrared, Ultraviolet, Microwaves, X-rays, Radio waves, Gamma rays.

---

### Solution

In the electromagnetic spectrum, wavelength is inversely proportional to frequency and energy. To list them from shortest wavelength (highest energy) to longest wavelength (lowest energy):

1.  **Gamma rays** (Shortest $\lambda$, highest frequency)
2.  **X-rays**
3.  **Ultraviolet**
4.  **Infrared**
5.  **Microwaves**
6.  **Radio waves** (Longest $\lambda$, lowest frequency)

---

### Final Result (Increasing Wavelength)
**Gamma rays < X-rays < Ultraviolet < Infrared < Microwaves < Radio waves**

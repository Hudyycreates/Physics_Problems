# 7. Wavelength and Frequency

### Problem Statement

The human eye is most sensitive to light with a wavelength of about **$550\text{ nm}$**. 
1. What color does this correspond to in the visible spectrum? 
2. What is the frequency of this light?

---

### Solution

#### 1. Identify the Color
A wavelength of $550\text{ nm}$ ($5.5 \times 10^{-7}\text{ m}$) falls right in the middle of the visible spectrum.

This corresponds to the color **Yellow-Green**.



[Image of visible light spectrum with wavelengths]


---

#### 2. Calculate the Frequency ($f$)
The relationship between speed, wavelength, and frequency is:
$$c = \lambda f$$

Where $c \approx 3.0 \times 10^8\text{ m/s}$. Rearranging for $f$:
$$f = \frac{c}{\lambda}$$

**Step A: Convert units**
$550\text{ nm} = 550 \times 10^{-9}\text{ m} = 5.5 \times 10^{-7}\text{ m}$

**Step B: Calculation**
$$f = \frac{3.0 \times 10^8}{5.5 \times 10^{-7}}$$
$$f \approx 5.45 \times 10^{14}\text{ Hz}$$

---

### Final Result
* **Color:** Yellow-Green
* **Frequency:** **$5.45 \times 10^{14}\text{ Hz}$** (or $545\text{ THz}$)
